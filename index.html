Our specialty biryani: A secret blend of spices, slow-cooked to perfection with locally sourced ingredients. A customer favorite with generations of expertise. Taste the magic today!

Chicken Tikka Masala: Succulent pieces of chicken marinated in a blend of aromatic spices, grilled to perfection, and simmered in a creamy tomato sauce. An irresistible dish that embodies the essence of Indian cuisine

Palak Paneer: A delectable North Indian dish featuring soft cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) immersed in a creamy spinach gravy. Nutrient-rich spinach and aromatic spices create a harmonious and flavorful vegetarian delight, perfect with naan or rice

Paneer Chilli: A popular Indo-Chinese fusion dish that features cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) tossed with colorful bell peppers and onions in a spicy, tangy sauce. It's a delightful balance of flavors and textures, making it a favorite appetizer or main course option.

Margherita Pizza: A classic Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil. It's a simple yet flavorful combination that pays homage to Queen Margherita of Italy.

Naan: A traditional Indian flatbread known for its soft and fluffy texture. It's typically baked in a tandoor oven and served as a versatile accompaniment to various Indian dishes, perfect for scooping up curries and sauces.
